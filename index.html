Ark Bangladesh

Preserving Nature's Best, Scientifically.

Ark Bangladesh is a modern agricultural farm. We collect higher-quality agricultural products directly from farmers and store them in a modern, scientific way to ensure their freshness and longevity. Our commitment is to quality, from the farm to your table.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between hard-working farmers and quality-conscious consumers, Ark Bangladesh is more than just a farm. We are a community dedicated to sustainable agriculture and advanced preservation techniques. We believe that by providing farmers with a reliable market and consumers with fresh, high-quality produce, we can create a healthier, more prosperous future for all. Our journey began with a simple idea: to treat agricultural products with the respect they deserve, ensuring their journey from soil to shelf is seamless and scientifically sound.

Our Product Portfolio

At Ark Bangladesh, we take pride in sourcing and preserving the finest agricultural products from local farms. Our modern storage solutions ensure that each product maintains its natural flavor, nutrients, and quality for longer.

Rice

As a staple food, our rice is sourced from the most fertile paddies across Bangladesh. We ensure a premium selection of varieties, each stored under optimal climate control to preserve its aroma, texture, and nutritional integrity. Our careful process guarantees that the rice you get is as fresh as if it was just harvested.

Potato

Our potatoes are known for their consistent quality and freshness. We collect them from trusted local farmers and store them in climate-controlled facilities to prevent sprouting and spoilage, ensuring they remain firm and flavorful. Perfect for every kitchen, from home cooking to industrial use.

Red Chilli

Sourced from the best farms, our red chillies are carefully selected and dried using modern techniques to maintain their vibrant color and intense heat. Our storage process ensures they retain their potency, making them a perfect addition to spice up any dish.

Peanut

We offer high-quality peanuts that are carefully dried and stored to preserve their natural crunch and flavor. They are ideal for various uses, from snacking to oil production, and are a rich source of protein and healthy fats.

Onion

Our onions are known for their strong flavor and long shelf life. We store them in a way that minimizes moisture and light exposure, ensuring they remain fresh, firm, and ready for use in any culinary application.
